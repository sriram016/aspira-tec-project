Silver Jubilee Legacy

Silver Jubilee & Legacy

In 2012, the school celebrated its Silver Jubilee, commemorating 25 years of educational service and social commitment. The souvenir released that year is a testament to the passion, perseverance, and unity that continues to drive the school and the Forum forward.

The journey of YWMS is proof that when youth unite with purpose, they can shape not only their futures but that of entire communities. As the banyan tree grows large with deep roots and strong branches, so too does YWMS thrive—nurtured not by individual glory, but by collective effort, sacrifice, and the divine blessings that have guided its mission.

Today, YWMS stands tall—not just as a school, but as a movement, a model, and a message: “Service to society is the highest form of education.”

About Us

In 1987, inspired by the visit of former President of India Dr. Zakir Husain to the village mosque inauguration years earlier, the Forum, with the generous support of Alhaj E.S.M. Bakker Mohamed, established the Dr. Zakir Husain English School. It began humbly with just 26 students in LKG and UKG.
