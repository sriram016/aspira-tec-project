Current Strength and Facilities

Today, YWMS proudly educates over 1,100 students, offering quality instruction from Kindergarten to Grade 12. It blends academic excellence with moral values and social responsibility, ensuring that students emerge as both learned and ethical citizens.

Smart classrooms
Computer and science laboratories
A well-stocked library
Playground and sports activities
Moral and co-curricular education
Regular career guidance and student counseling

About Us

In 1987, inspired by the visit of former President of India Dr. Zakir Husain to the village mosque inauguration years earlier, the Forum, with the generous support of Alhaj E.S.M. Bakker Mohamed, established the Dr. Zakir Husain English School. It began humbly with just 26 students in LKG and UKG.
